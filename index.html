Who Are We?

Family Dental was founded in 1919 and is one of Ottawa's longest standing dental clinics. We have been voted #1 dental clinic in Ottawa 5 times. We strive to provide our patients top of the line care at affordable prices. Our doctors have over 50 years of experience and are trusted by the community.

We provide a wide array of services ranging from pediatric dentistry to oral surgery. We are also involved in community programs such as Teeth for Kids and Healthy Smile.
